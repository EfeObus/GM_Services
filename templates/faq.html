Account Management

Creating an account is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  1. Click the "Register" button on our homepage
  2. Fill in your personal information (name, email, phone)
  3. Choose a strong password
  4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
  5. Complete your profile with additional details

Once verified, you'll have access to all our services and can start making bookings immediately.

For account verification, you'll need to provide:

  • Government-issued ID: National ID, Passport, or Driver's License
  • Proof of Address: Utility bill or bank statement (within 3 months)
  • Phone Verification: We'll send a verification code to your mobile
  • Email Confirmation: Click the link sent to your email address

Verification typically takes 24-48 hours and is required for financial services and high-value transactions.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Log in with your new password

If you don't receive the email within 15 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
