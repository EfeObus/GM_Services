Please read our loan terms and conditions carefully before applying

1. Introduction

Welcome to GM Services Loan Program. This Loan Policy and Agreement ("Agreement") governs the terms and conditions under which GM Services ("we," "us," or "our") provides loan services to eligible customers ("you" or "borrower") in Nigeria.

By applying for a loan through our platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement.

3. Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a loan, you must meet the following requirements:

General Requirements
  • Must be a Nigerian citizen or legal resident
  • Age between 21 and 65 years
  • Valid Bank Verification Number (BVN)
  • Active Nigerian bank account
  • Verifiable source of income
  • Good credit history (if applicable)
Income Requirements
  • Minimum monthly income of ₦100,000
  • Stable employment for at least 6 months
  • Debt-to-income ratio below 40%
  • 3 months bank statements
  • Employment letter or business registration

4. Required Documents

Standard Documents
  • Valid government-issued ID (National ID, Driver's License, or International Passport)
  • BVN verification
  • 3 months bank statements
  • Proof of income (salary slip, business income certificate)
  • Utility bill (proof of address)
Additional Documents
  • For loans above ₦1,000,000: Collateral documents
  • For auto loans: Employment letter or business registration
  • For business loans: Business plan and financial statements
  • Self-employed: Tax certificates and business registration

All documents must be uploaded in PDF format with a maximum file size of 5MB each.

5. Loan Processing and Approval

Application Submission

Complete online application with required documents

Initial Review (24-48 hours)

Document verification and eligibility check

Credit Assessment (2-5 business days)

Income verification and credit evaluation

Final Decision

Approval notification and loan offer

Processing time may vary based on loan amount, complexity, and completeness of documentation. We reserve the right to request additional information or documentation during the review process.

6. Repayment Terms

Payment Schedule
  • Monthly repayments on a fixed date
  • First payment due 30 days after disbursement
  • Payments via direct debit or bank transfer
  • Grace period of 5 days after due date
Early Repayment
  • No penalty for early repayment
  • Interest calculated only for utilized period
  • Partial prepayments allowed
  • 30-day notice required for full prepayment

8. Default and Collections

Default Consequences
  • Loan considered in default after 90 days of non-payment
  • Negative credit bureau reporting
  • Collection agency engagement
  • Legal action for debt recovery
  • Collateral enforcement (if applicable)

We encourage borrowers facing financial difficulties to contact us immediately to discuss restructuring options before defaulting on payments.
