Please read these terms carefully before applying for any loan facility

1. Introduction and Definitions

1.1 Introduction

These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern the provision of loan services by GM Services Nigeria Limited ("GM Services", "we", "us", "our") to eligible borrowers ("you", "borrower", "customer") within the Federal Republic of Nigeria. These Terms are binding upon acceptance of a loan offer and disbursement of funds.

1.2 Definitions
Term Definition
Loan Agreement The contract between GM Services and the borrower for a specific loan facility
Principal Amount The initial amount of money lent to the borrower, excluding interest and fees
Interest Rate The percentage charged annually on the outstanding loan balance using reducing balance method
Tenor The agreed period for loan repayment, expressed in months

Collateral Assets pledged as security for loan repayment

2.2 Interest Calculation

Interest is calculated using the reducing balance method where interest is charged only on the outstanding principal balance. The effective annual percentage rate (APR) includes all applicable fees and charges as mandated by the Central Bank of Nigeria.

Monthly Payment Formula

Monthly Payment = [P × r × (1 + r)^n] / [(1 + r)^n - 1]
Where: P = Principal, r = Monthly interest rate, n = Number of months

3. Eligibility and Application Requirements

3.1 General Eligibility
  • Nigerian citizen or legal resident with valid identification
  • Age between 21 and 65 years at loan maturity
  • Minimum monthly income of ₦100,000 (verifiable)
  • Valid Bank Verification Number (BVN)
  • Active Nigerian domiciliary bank account
  • Good credit history and no current default on other facilities
  • Debt-to-income ratio not exceeding 40%
3.2 Additional Requirements by Loan Type
Business Loans
  • Valid CAC business registration
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  • Business bank statements (12 months)
  • Business plan and financial projections
  • Collateral for amounts above ₦1,000,000
Auto Loans
  • Vehicle acts as primary collateral
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage
  • Vehicle inspection certificate
  • Employment verification letter

4. Repayment Terms and Conditions

4.1 Repayment Schedule
  • Monthly installments due on the same date each month
  • First payment due 30 days after loan disbursement
  • Payments via direct debit, bank transfer, or cash deposit
  • Grace period of 5 business days after due date without penalty
  • Payments applied first to fees, then interest, then principal
4.2 Payment Methods
Preferred Methods
  • Standing order/Direct debit
  • Online bank transfer
  • Mobile banking apps
  • USSD banking codes
Alternative Methods
  • Bank branch deposits
  • ATM transfers
  • Authorized payment agents
  • Post-dated cheques (where applicable)
4.3 Early Repayment

Borrowers may repay loans early without penalty. Early repayment reduces total interest charges, and interest is calculated only for the actual period of utilization. A 30-day written notice is required for full prepayment to facilitate administrative processes.

6. Default and Consequences

6.1 Events of Default
  • Failure to make scheduled payments for 90 consecutive days
  • Providing false or misleading information in loan application
  • Using loan funds for purposes other than declared
  • Disposal of collateral without lender consent
  • Borrower's death, insolvency, or bankruptcy
  • Material adverse change in borrower's financial condition
  • Breach of any covenant or condition in the loan agreement
6.2 Consequences of Default
Immediate Consequences
  • Entire loan balance becomes immediately due and payable
  • Additional default interest rate of 3% per month
  • Negative reporting to credit bureaus (CREDICORP, CRC Credit Bureau)
  • Restriction from accessing future credit facilities
  • Collection agency engagement
  • Legal action for debt recovery
  • Realization of collateral security
6.3 Recovery Process
30-60 Days Past Due

Reminder notices, phone calls, restructuring options

60-90 Days Past Due

Formal demand letters, credit bureau reporting

90+ Days Past Due

Default declaration, collection agency, legal action
